About Me

Technology and its advancement has always fascinated me. I have always loved reading, learning and exploring more about it. However, in this huge expanse of 'technology', my front runner has always been 'Android Development'. I have been learning Android from the past 2 years and done a bunch of app projects as well. Furthermore, I am also conversant with Facebook SDK and UI designing using Adobe XD. Through all these experiences, I have:

  • Cultivated a good understanding of MVP and MVVM architecture.
  • Gained knowledge of Firebase, Firestore, GCM
  • Acquired the know-how of Material design principles
